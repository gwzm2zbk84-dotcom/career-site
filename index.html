About

The operator behind the curtain.

My career has been built on taking messy, undefined problems — the ones with no existing playbook — and building the process or system that puts them to rest. DOJ subpoena with two billion records. Azure data center migration with 1,200+ test cases. AI agents that eliminated manual governance busywork. Same pattern every time.

I've led teams of business analysts, project managers, and data scientists, and I'm equally comfortable as a hands-on individual contributor. What's constant is a data-first mindset: I understand how data flows, where it breaks, and how to structure it so decisions are trustworthy.

I'm now bringing that operational depth into high-growth technology and AI infrastructure environments where enterprise rigor and speed of iteration need to coexist.
